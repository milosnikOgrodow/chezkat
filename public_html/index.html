Our Story

Welcome to Chez Kat, where culinary magic happens. Founded in 2010 by Kat herself, a passionate chef with a love for local ingredients and bold flavors, we've been on a mission to deliver unforgettable dining experiences. Our journey began with a small brunch cafe and has since expanded into a full-service catering business, delighting guests at events of all sizes with our signature dishes and impeccable service.
